theory

LATTICE: The geometrical form consisting only of a regular. array of points in space is called a lattice.

CRYSTAL: A crystal is a homogeneous portion of a solid substance made of a regular pattern of structural units bonded by plane surfaces making definite angles with each other.

BASIS:
The crystal structure is formed. associating every lattice point with an assembly of atoms or molecules or ions, which are identical in composition, arrangement and orientation is called as the basis.
Thus, 
lattice + basis = crystal structure
